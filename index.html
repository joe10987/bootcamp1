For all your transport needs there is always Hope!

Gurney Transports

We are the only non-emergency company that uses ambulance staffed with EMTs. This option is best for your loved ones whom are unable to stand and pivot. Another reason this option would be best is if your loved one is being transported over a long distance.

Wheelchair/Gerichair Transports

Wheelchair transports are best for patients whom can stand and pivot; but cannot walk.

Gerichair are specialized wheelchairs that accommodate patients who cannot tollerate sitting at a 90°.

Convalescent Car

This type of transport is best suited for patients whom ambulate(walk) with little to no assistance.
